Welcome to AI Crop Disease Prediction

Detect crop diseases early using Artificial Intelligence and Image Processing.

Project Overview

This project uses AI and Deep Learning techniques to identify crop diseases from leaf images. Farmers can upload images and receive disease predictions along with treatment suggestions.

Problem Statement

Farmers often face difficulties identifying crop diseases at an early stage. Delayed detection leads to crop damage and lower productivity.

Objectives

  • Detect crop diseases using AI
  • Provide early prediction
  • Reduce crop losses
  • Improve agricultural productivity
